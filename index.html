Employment, Contract and Payment Plan

For Term 1

Note: This plan is the very first version of the payroll and contract plan. The plan comes into action once the company has its first 100 customers.

Content Dept  

The content department will be paid on per course basis.

There will be no senior position in the content dept. And it will directly be managed by the CEO for the first few courses.

The dept shall be paid 40 percent of the total revenue that the course generates for its first 100 customers.

The revenue that has been allotted to the content dept. Shall be divided and paid to its members based on the following factors:

  1. WorkLoad
  2. Quality of Work
  3. Type of content

The one with a higher workload, good quality of work, and producing the most needed content will be given a 60% share of the total received by the Dept. and the other to be paid the rest 40%.

E.g:         course valued at 1000/-

Revenue generated for first 100 sales : 1,00,000

40 % of total Revenue = 40k

Responsibilities :

  1. Researching the given topic properly
  2. Coming up with the best content from the research.
  3. Decorating the content with good English, Audio recording or Video making as needed
  4. Providing content for social media handles. E.g: memes, Videos

Contract Term: mostly Active all time

Development Dept - Front end

The development department will be paid for the lifecycle of the development of the website.

The developer with the highest quality of work will be retained with the company for maintenance of the site and any future work.

The Dept will be paid 40k+ for the entire life cycle of development. This payment will be divided into milestones and be paid only after each milestone is completed successfully.

The Dept shall have 2 positions.

  1. Senior
  2. Junior

The Senior developer will be paid 50% of what’s coming into the dept. And the rest will be divided equally to the junior devs in the team.

Further clarification will be provided to the dept as we go further

Responsibilities :

  1. Developing the Front-end with the given wireframes.
  2. Developing securely and attention to detail.
  3. Reviewing the site and making necessary changes if needed.
  4. Planning the entire front end.

Contract Term: until the Development cycle, best dev will be retained for maintenance

Editing Dept

The Editing dept will be paid 10k / campaign.

The dept may or may not have a junior editor. Which will be decided according to the workload of the current Editor.

In case there exists a Jr. Editor. The senior will hold 60% of the total what’s coming in.  and the rest will be divided equally among the juniors.

Responsibilities :

  1. Editing content for social media handles.
  2. Course content editing.

Contract Term: mostly Active all time

Marketing Dept.

The dept will be paid on a performance basis. The manager whose channel brings the most revenue will be given 20% of the revenue share.

The marketing dept will of course contain multiple channels.

Each channel will have an individual. And be paid as mentioned above.

Contract Term: mostly Active all time from the launch of site

Responsibilities :

  1. Intense research on marketing
  2. Applying the best marketing techniques and sales funnels.
  3. Managing the funnels and running in the budget allotted

Sales Dept.

Perhaps, the most valuable. The sales Dept will be getting a direct commission of 40 percent on every course that is sold.

Responsibilities :

  1. Direct selling the courses.
  2. Maintaining good relations with customers
  3. Acquiring long term customers.
  4. Finding new customers and markets actively

Contract Term: mostly Active all time

Research Dept.

The dept will be paid a bonus of 5k every campaign for the excellent work they did or will do.

The payment is absolutely based on the performance and new ideas they come up with.

Responsibilities :

  1. Coming up with the best plans for the company regarding any dept
  2. Making proper working plans for the highest efficiency of the people

Contract Term: mostly Active all time

As mentioned at the very beginning of this document. This is the very first version of the Payment plan. And this is bound to vary as we go further into the development process. The key factors that will affect the changes in this plan will be

Delivering best results with excellent performance. 

In which case, the payment for the person may be increased or they may be promoted to the senior position.

Not Delivering as expected or up to company standards.

In which case, they, unfortunately, will be considered to be removed from the   team.

Not being responsive to the group or team.

In which case, they will be considered for a talk with the internal team and be   removed necessary.

It is to be noted that every member who adds extra value to the company by

  1. Showing high interest in the business.
  2. Taking up additional responsibilities
  3. Delivering on time
  4. Delivering Quality work.
  5. Comes up with new ideas and plans for the team

Will surely be of high value to the company. And would be considered for joining them as a business partner and board member of the company. The company will always appreciate the one who is eager to get new skills and believes in the company’s vision.
